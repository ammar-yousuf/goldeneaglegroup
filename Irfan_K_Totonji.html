Irfan K. Totonji, President

Irfan K. Totonji, President

As the President of Golden Eagle Group, Mr. Totonji is responsible for the day-to-day operations of Golden Eagle Group. He personally heads the Golden Eagle Management Team and directly oversees the trades and home building activities of Golden Eagle Group.

Mr. Totonji holds a Bachelors Degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Petroleum and Minerals in Dhahran, Saudi Arabia and an MBA in Business Administration from Strayer University near Washington DC. His Arabic origin and long experience with American business concepts, together with his fluency in Arabic and English, provide him with the skills and the background to understand cultural differences and effectively communicate with a broad client base.

Since arriving in the United States in 1988, Mr. Totonji, working with Hadid Development Company, was directly responsible for the development and construction of significant commercial office buildings in the Washington DC area. Those projects contain more than 5 million square feet of space and are valued in excess of $450 million.

Golden Eagle Group has also developed and/or built more than 750 homes in over 20 communities and off-sites throughout the Washington DC metropolitan area. Mr. Totonji has an extensive network of professional business leaders throughout the world. He is also active in community service and politics.
