new communities




Heritage Hamlet, a scenic new community of only nine exclusive home sites, is ideally situated just south of Leesburg's historic downtown and just north of the famed Oatlands Plantation. This unique property, the last "hamlet" to be built in Loudoun County, features eight 1-acre home sites, a 37-acre conservancy home site, two pristine lakes and mountain views, all in a peaceful country setting.

Best of all, Heritage Hamlet offers these incredible home sites and amenities so close to so much. The community is off Gleedsville Road, a few minutes south of Leesburg's charming downtown district with its quaint restaurants, shops and galleries. And just minutes away are the Leesburg Premium Outlets and all the area's main arteries of travel – Route 15, Route 7, Route 267 (The Greenway), Route 50 and the Dulles International Airport. Heritage Hamlet is located in one of the most highly rated school districts in Loudoun County.

Yet for all this convenient proximity to so much, the residents of Heritage Hamlet can arrive back home to a serene community with quiet, relaxing evenings enjoying tranquil sunsets and spectacular night time star gazing, far from city lights and the din of traffic. You experience the openness of an expansive lot but only have to maintain your 1-acre home site…the best of all worlds!



Golden Eagle Development Group is planning to offer several home styles ranging in sizes from 3,000 to 4,000 square feet with impressive standard features, a wide range of options and customizing opportunities and sale prices expected to start in the low $800,000's.

The conservancy home site presents a multitude of wonderful opportunities. This unique property has its own separate entrance off Gleedsville Road featuring historic circular stone walls connected by an iron gate and a long winding driveway. The existing guest residence is a large two-story brick home with front and rear porches, a gourmet kitchen and has both lake and mountain views. The guesthouse can be renovated as a main residence or remain a guesthouse while a new main residence is built. The huge red barn can be utilized for a multitude of storage or business purposes. This property even has its own standby generator in the event of power outages. Golden Eagle stands ready to accommodate the wishes of purchasers for this versatile and unique property site.

Development of the Heritage Hamlet site has now begun and new home construction is anticipated for the Spring of 2013.
