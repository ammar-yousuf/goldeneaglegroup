ABOUT US

The Golden Eagle Group consists of a number of corporations and partnerships (both limited and general) organized for the purpose of investing in the United States.

These companies all began operating in 1991. Our major emphasis, in investments to date, has been real estate acquisition, development and construction. Our projects have included development and construction of over
$1 Billion in commercial properties, as well as hotels and mixed use residential projects.

Golden Eagle Group's corporate headquarters are located in Herndon, Virginia approximately 20 miles outside of Washington DC. Our employees are engaged in investment feasibility analysis, project acquisition, land planning and development, quality house designs and customizations, financing and accounting. We have employees on our jobsites who specialize in property development, construction and sales. Golden Eagle Group maintains full management responsibility for all its land development and construction activities.

From our very beginnings, effort has always been made to concentrate on developing relationships with individuals and companies that have demonstrated excellence and expertise in their fields. We have created an extensive network of trusted bankers, engineers, land planners and construction professionals, all of who share Golden Eagle Group's commitment to honesty and integrity. Our investor partners and our customers reap the rewards of working with such a trusted network.

Golden Eagle Group maintains a commitment to quality that is evident by the success of our investments, the beauty of our homes and communities and the satisfaction of our customers. Over the years, our return on investment has most often exceeded the expectations of our Board of Directors and our management team. We have established high standards of excellence in investment management and business development.

We strive to provide only the highest level of professional service to our customers and investors.



Our Corporate Management

 

Irfan K. Totonji, President

As the President of Golden Eagle Group, Mr. Totonji is responsible for the day-to-day operations of Golden Eagle Group. He personally heads the Golden Eagle Management Team and directly oversees the trades and home building activities of Golden Eagle Group.

Mr. Totonji holds a Bachelors Degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Petroleum and Minerals in Dhahran, Saudi Arabia and an MBA in Business Administration from Strayer University near Washington DC. His Arabic origin and long experience with American business concepts, together with his fluency in Arabic and English, provide him with the skills and the background to understand cultural differences and effectively communicate with a broad client base.

Since arriving in the United States in 1988, Mr. Totonji, working with Hadid Development Company, was directly responsible for the development and construction of significant commercial office buildings in the Washington DC area. Those projects contain more than 5 million square feet of space and are valued in excess of $450 million.

Golden Eagle Group has also developed and/or built more than 750 homes in over 20 communities and off-sites throughout the Washington DC metropolitan area. Mr. Totonji has an extensive network of professional business leaders throughout the world. He is also active in community service and politics.

Ghazwa Alyounes, Vice President

As the Vice President of Golden Eagle Development Group, Mrs. Alyounes has the primary responsibility of managing strategic planning and operations.  She is a member of Golden Eagle’s executive management team and provides invaluable input into all investment decisions. Among her responsibilities, Mrs. Alyounes oversees accounting and financial functions, including planning, budgeting and cash flow analysis. 

Mrs. Alyounes has a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Information from Strayer University in Virginia.
